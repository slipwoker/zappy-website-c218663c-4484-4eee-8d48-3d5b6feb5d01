    Meet Yoav and Ariel Gindi - Professional DJs bringing the best trance music

    Yoav and Ariel Gindi are two brothers who share a deep passion for trance, techno, and house music. Our story began several years ago when we fell in love with electronic music and realized we wanted to make it our profession. Since then, we've been working tirelessly to bring the best musical experience to young people in Israel.

    Our journey in the world of trance music wasn't easy - we started with small performances at private parties, learned the art of mixing and building sets, and invested many hours in learning professional equipment. Today, we're proud to be professional DJs performing at private events, large parties, and festivals throughout the country.

    What sets us apart is not only our experience and professionalism, but also our genuine love for music and the people who come to our events. We see ourselves not just as DJs, but as experience creators - we want every event to be a special memory that lasts over time.

    Our Mission

    To bring the best trance music to young people in Israel and create unforgettable musical experiences. We believe that music can connect people, evoke emotions, and create a special atmosphere that makes everyone feel part of something bigger.

    Our Vision

    To become the leading DJs in the field of trance music in Israel and continue to develop and innovate. We want to bring the most modern sound, use the most advanced technology, and create sets that combine different styles in a unique and original way.

    Our Values

    Passion for music

    Trance music isn't just a profession for us - it's a real passion. We live and breathe music, and this energy is felt in every performance.

    Professionalism

    We take every event seriously and bring the best equipment, the most comprehensive preparation, and the most professional execution.

    Creativity

    Every set we build is unique and original. We don't use ready-made sets but create new mixes that are perfectly suited to the event.

    Connection with the audience

    We know our audience - young people aged 15-25 who love energetic music. We know what makes them dance and how to create the perfect atmosphere.

    Our Experience and Expertise

    Over the years, we've gained extensive experience performing at different types of events - from small private parties to large festivals with hundreds of participants. We know the most advanced mixing techniques, know how to build a set that starts soft and ends with high energy, and understand the dynamics of a young audience.

    Our expertise includes not only trance music, but also techno and house - three styles that we know how to combine harmoniously and excitingly. We use the most advanced professional DJ equipment, including digital controllers, advanced lighting systems, and quality sound systems that ensure the music sounds perfect.

    What also sets us apart is our ability to adapt to any type of event - whether it's an intimate birthday party, a large beach party, or a professional trance festival. We know how to read the crowd, feel the energy, and adjust the set accordingly to create the perfect experience.
