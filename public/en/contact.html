    Frequently asked questions

    How far in advance do I need to book?

    We recommend booking at least two weeks in advance, but we're happy to try to accommodate more urgent events as well. The earlier you book, the more time we'll have to plan the perfect set.

    What equipment do you bring?

    We come with all the professional equipment - DJ controllers, high-quality sound system, advanced lighting and more. We make sure everything is ready and works perfectly.

    Can I request specific songs?

    Absolutely! We love incorporating special requests into our set. Tell us what you like and we'll build a set that combines your preferences with our professional style.

    How long do you perform?

    Performance length varies according to the type of event - usually between 2-4 hours. We'd be happy to talk with you and adjust the performance length exactly to your needs.
